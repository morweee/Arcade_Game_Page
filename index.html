Matching Game

Game Category: Core Developer Skills

Game Info: A memory card game in which
two covered cards are flipped face up over each turn.
The objective is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

You will be exploring and discovering the appearance of various unknown planets and their diplopia!

As a space traveler, It is your task to record all planets' locations by observing and memorizing... MATCH!

Escape the planet!

Runner Game

Game Category: Fundemental Concepts

Game Info: runner game in which the player controls a character who runs through an endless path.
The player must jump over enemies to avoid crash.

You've decided to land on a planet of desert --- Akkaris, to explore this weird surrounding.

But, you are now chased by the local monsters. You task is to escape from them and return to your spaceship... RUN!

Defending Aliens!

Space Invaders

Game Category: Open Source Game

Game Info: A space invader game in which
the player controls a spaceship to move horizontally at the bottom of the page, firing missiles up at the aliens above.
The player must shoot and destroy all moving enemies.

As your space travel in this galaxy comes to an end, you are ready and excited for a new exploration.

But, the local aliens are not happy with your presence. They are chasing you down. You must defend yourself and your spaceship... SHOOT!
