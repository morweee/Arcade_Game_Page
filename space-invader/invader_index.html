Press the "Start Game" button to play the game.

The invaders are moving down the screen from the top,
and you have to shoot to destroy them before they reach the bottom.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the shooter,
and press the up arrow key to shoot.

The game is over if the invaders reach the bottom of the screen.
